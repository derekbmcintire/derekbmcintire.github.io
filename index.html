SpokeRetriever

An app to help people find their lost bicycles

SpokeRetriever is a bicycle registration site to help users keep track of their bicycles. Users have the option to create, edit, remove, and view their bikes. Bikes can also be registered, marked stolen or recovered, and non-registered users can view all bicycles and see their status. The front end was built with JavaScript/jQuery, Bootstrap and SASS, and the RESTful API it relies on with Ruby on Rails and a PostgreSQL database.
