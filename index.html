Hi, I'm Derek!

Developer. Teacher. Photographer. Cyclist. Traveler.

What I love about programming is that it satisfies my need for a constant challenge, and I get the opportunity to create something that benefits others.

I’m an inspired programmer with years of experience working at social justice organizations. I was drawn to programming through a drive to create and problem solve, and decided to pursue a career in web development because I believe I can use it to build and contribute to meaningful projects that can address real-world needs.

Through web development, I have learned that a huge part of programming is knowing how to fail, and that failing is often times a step forward in the process. Having this perspective has allowed me to not only write the best code I can, but to build a strong foundation in programming, regardless of language or technology used. Going forward, I’m eager to dive deeper into both front and backend development, get more experience with pairing and teams, and make a meaningful contribution to larger projects.
