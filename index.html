Hi, I'm Derek!

Developer. Teacher. Photographer. Cyclist. Traveler.

What I love about programming is that it satisfies my need for a constant challenge, and I get the opportunity to create something that benefits others.

I'm an inspired programmer with years of experience working at social justice centered organizations locally and internationally. In the past I've been a bicycle mechanic and program coordinator in Boston, a teacher in South Korea and a field worker in Ghana. My mechanics background has been particularly relevant in my journey so-far through programming, and I've relied heavily on my problem solving experience to help bring my ideas from the whiteboard to the web.

As a fifth generation Dorchester resident, I've seen the city transform over the last three decades while affordable housing, income inequality and reliable transportation remain major issues. Still, I'm an optimist who believes our actions and choices matter, and those of us who have opportunities to affect change have a responsibility to act.

I see programming as a vehicle through which I can put my ideas into action. I want to collaborate with other members of my community and contribute to projects that are serving their needs.

An app to help people find their lost bicycles
